Electronics and Computer Engineering

The Electronics and Computer Engineering programme has been designed in such a way so as to incorporate two very separate engineering fields and put them together to meet the immense demands of industries from across the world.
The first two years of the course majorly involve the topics of Electronics and Communication Engineering and the remaining course offers a variety of subjects related to Computer Science and Programming to provide the students with the best of both worlds!
The joint applications; such as, mobile phones, space shuttles, desktops, tablets, etc are few of the examples where a competence in both of the fields is of paramount importance.
Throughout this course, students will not only get an opportunity to work on various communication networks, data transmission but also understand the functioning and designing of computing systems.
