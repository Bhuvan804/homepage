BRANCHES

Branches that come under the department

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is perhaps one of the most diverse and versatile branches of engineering, and for ones with interest in designing, innovations, inventions, and developments, it is a domain without limits and being one of the oldest and most reputed branches of the institute, it surely does have a lot to offer.

This field constitutes the study of machinery and their design and how to manufacture efficient and optimum products. The applications of this field are never-ending. One could work on creating robots, airplanes, hybrid and autonomous cars, rapid prototyping and 3D printing, reverse engineering, robotics & automation, and so much more.



Mechanical Engineering(Production)

The increase in the demand of engineers in the field of industrial production with relevant knowledge of functioning and optimizing production created the need for this branch of engineering. The Production engineers are capable of supervising the entire production system of a facility, optimizing a production line, managing manufacturing costs, etc. This field also provides the necessary design, production, and automation skills required in Mechanical Engineering.



Mechatronics

Mechatronics is another branch which comes under the Mechanical Engineering Department. This field of engineering includes a combination of various engineering disciplines such as mechanical, electrical, telecommunication, and computer engineering. It mainly focuses on putting together the principles of mechanics, electronics circuits and programming to automate the machines and generate more efficient and reliable systems. Control systems, Automation, and Robotics are key focus areas in this branch.
