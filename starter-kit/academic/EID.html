BRANCHES

Branches that come under the department

Electrical Engineering

In today’s digital day and age, electricity is truly what keeps the world running. Electrical Engineering is a branch that focuses on designing, developing, and maintaining these vital electrical machineries and systems. With today’s dependence on electricity and technology, the opportunities in this field are quite promising and genuinely endless.
This field allows one to work on Power Generation and Distribution, Smart Grid, Electric Vehicles, Smart Electrical Networks, and much more.



Electrical and Computer Engineering

Electrical and Computer Engineering is a newly introduced programme that comes under the Electrical and Instrumentation Department of Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology.
The entire course structure has been curated in such a way that students have the opportunity to explore and understand topics like Smart Infrastructure (Electric grid, SCADA, IoT solutions), Energy Systems and Transportation, Flexible and Secure Storage, Real-Time Computation, Algorithms, and Data Science.



Biomedical Engineering

A unique, interdisciplinary field that bridges the gap between engineering and medicine, seamlessly by combining the problem-solving skills of engineering with medical and biological sciences to improve healthcare diagnosis, monitoring, and therapy.
The primary focus of this program involves the application, control and designing of medical-purpose instruments and equipment to create prominent breakthroughs in the development of healthcare devices and keep making improvements in their applications.



Electronics (Instrumentation & Control) Engineering

This branch of Electronics (Instrumentation & Control) Engineering encompasses the designing and controlling of various systems. Its prime area of focus involves automation and control of general-purpose instruments and equipment that are used in the industries. This innovative branch provides expertise in various fields and also focuses on topics like Automation and Robotics.
