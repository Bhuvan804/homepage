FOOD JOINTS

Welcome to the Foodie World. For the ones who’ve got a soft spot for food inside them, gear up because Thapar brings to you the best of it.
Food may be essential as fuel for the body, but good food is fuel for the soul. The several food joints at Thapar not only satisfy hunger but also provide a spot for students to chill with your friends and create new memories, capturing laughter within the walls.

Food Joints

Food joints are spread across every corner in the campus & here’s a list of some of them:

COS Complex




  • Pizza Nation: One of the “pizza over anyone” kind of person? This is the right place for you.
  • Wrapchik: Heavenly burgers, wraps and mocktails that would soon turn into an addiction.
  • Sips and Bites: Missing ‘maa ke haath ka khaana?’ Try sips and bites. Enjoy toothsome Indian food.
  • Bombay Munchery: Any south Indian foodie around? It’s the place for you.
  • Juju’s cafe: A perfect place to sit and gossip while sipping on to the best mocktails in campus.


Jaggi




Famished after hours of classes? One can get the yummiest shakes, healthy juices and maggi with one’s best buddies. This place gives the most vibrant vibes and is the perfect spot for evening chit- chats converting into a lifetime of memories.



The Main Cafeteria

Mouth-watering cholle bhature, paranthas, evening snacks of cakes and chocolate brownies are always on one’s checklist. It is the perfect destination for birthday parties.



G block canteen

For all chai addicts, enjoy a quick tea and rajma chawal. One must try all varieties of rolls and momos from the stalls. They are always serving to curb all late night desires.



Library canteen

This is the place where great ideas and bad decisions are born. A place one can hop at during late night study group session. The consistent need for caffeine will be fulfilled here.



Iqbal juices

A place where everybody’s parents would want them to be at. The attendance here will always be greater than that in the classrooms. This place will provide students with a variety of invigorating juices and fruits.



Main Gate Stalls

This place promises everybody the perfect crunch of momos, maggi, omelette and tea.



Aahar

Famous for its samosa, bhel puri and chai , AHAAR caters the food craving of crowds ranging from teachers and students taking a short break before their lectures. In the hustle, can be heard, the struggling stories of pending assignment submissions, attendance and the tiring classes.



Night Canteens

A foodie and a night owl? Got a birthday party to celebrate at midnight? Do not worry! Night canteens have it all covered. These hotspots are within the hostel premises to provide all the snacks and dishes to curb late night hunger. Never hesitate to bond with fellow hostellers over that 'late night coffee'.





Lastly, Don’t Worry Juniors!

No matter what, Food at thapar won’t let anyone down!
