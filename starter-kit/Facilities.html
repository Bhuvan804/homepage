Medical

Medical

The institute has a well equipped and fully functional health care centre with doctors and nursing agents to offer medical care for minor issues and first aid. Other salient features include:

  • Ambulance on call. (24*7)
  • Proper counselling before dispensing of medicine.
  • Health education and nutrition/diet advice.
  • Every hostel also has a basic first aid kit along with a Medical secretary.
  • The institute also has tie-ups with various hospitals in Patiala.
Dispensary timings are:
  • 8:30 am to 7:30 pm. (Summer)
  • 8:30 am to 7:00 pm. ( Winter)
  • And 10 pm to 6 am during nights.

Hostel

Hostel



The most cherished memories in college life are created in the home away from home, the hostels. Therefore, Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology looks forward to providing the students with all the facilities that would aid them, so that it can be an experience to relish forever.
Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology has a total of 14 hostels situated within the campus, out of which 9 are for boys and 5 are for girls. The hostels are surrounded by majestic lawns that encompass beauty of all sorts. The proximity to nature is to such an extent that the mornings in the hostels can be enjoyed with the sighting of peacocks and numerous other birds. Apart from this, all the hostels provide basic facilities like high speed internet connection, R.O water purifiers, bathrooms with 24x7 water supply and installed with geysers, a TV room/Common room, a hostel mess ensuring hygienic and nutritious diet and much more. Apart from these facilities, the students are provided with two laundry days in a week. Each hostel also has a reading room just in case one’s fatigue prevents him/her from walking to the central library. Talking about fatigue, each hostel has a gymnasium equipped with modern machinery and other indoor game facilities including table tennis, carom, chess and also badminton courts. Night canteens are also available (within the hostel itself) which provide “post dinner” meals to curb those late night food cravings.


Further, all the hostels have fine security measures which include CCTVs, guards at the entrance and patrolling in the hostel and fire alarms inside the premises. For girl’s hostels, security guards are posted at the hostel gate 24x7 and are responsible for accompanying them to the library for late night studies. The staff is cooperative and vigilant, and ensures a safe environment for the students to learn and have fun simultaneously.
Students also have gala time celebrating various functions and festivals in the hostel like Diwali night, Independence Day, Republic day, Hostel night and Lohri function.
So brace yourselves, for this is going to be the golden period of your lives!

Sports

Sports


“Every champion was once a contender that refused to give up.”

Sports are an indispensable part of education at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology. Sports help in strengthening character and develop the importance of discipline in life. They inculcate a feeling of respect, and also make one learn the value of self-control. Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology has a specially developed sports environment that matches international standards and gives a truly global experience to all the students.

TIET provides students with the best sports facilities.

Athletics:
Thapar has a well maintained 400m IAAF approved Synthetic Athletic track.

Cricket:
There are two lush green fully maintained cricket grounds ready for the game with fully automated sprinkler systems and white painted fences. Not just that, there is a cemented and a turf net to develop the skills.

Football:
There are two lush green full-size football fields, and practice takes place under the watchful eye of expert coaches.

Basketball:
Thapar has one synthetic and two cemented basketball courts, of which one is reserved for girls to practice.

Badminton:
Along with an indoor synthetic court, there are nets in the open for all time use.

Tennis:
Institute has four synthetic lawn tennis courts for trained players, and two cemented courts and a practice wall for beginners.

Swimming:
Thapar has a standard pool and a well-qualified coach to train the students.
