Patiala

About

Patiala, the epicentre of Punjab boasts of a perfect melange of history, culture, food and craft. It may not be a very urban space, especially for students hailing from the metro cities, but its rustic vibe is equally endearing.

Nearest Airport

The nearest airport that can be used by civilians, is the Chandigarh Airport, at a distance of about 64 kms from the city of Patiala

Nearest Bus Stop

The Patiala Bus Stop can help you hitch rides to several locations including Chandigarh, Delhi, Ludhiana, Sangrur etc, with a 24x7 availability of running buses.

Netaji Subhas National Institute of Sports



Netaji Subhas National Institute of Sports, Patiala, India which is also Asia's largest Sports Institute built by the erstwhile Maharaja of Patiala whose descendants dedicated this complex for the promotion of sports to the people of India. This Institute is popularly known as the "Mecca" of Indian Sports and has produced Coaches of high caliber. It has various facilities that you can check out like gymnasium and swimming pool complex.
Three indoor halls for wrestling, weightlifting, boxing, basketball, badminton, table tennis, volleyball, handball and judo.
It also has:

  • Two squash courts
  • Cycling velodrome
  • Hockey field astroturf and three grass fields
  • Athletic tracks
  • Two football grounds
  • Turf wicket cricket ground and 6 cricket nets
  • Wushu Training Centre
  • Fencing training Centre
and many more.
It is one of the must visit places for all the sport freaks.

Kali Devi Temple

Dedicated to goddess Kali, this temple sees hundreds of devotees making a beeline for it, particularly on Saturdays. The shrine’s most treasured possession is a collection of manuscript leaves, believed to have been written by Rishi Vyas, the composer of the epic Mahabharata.

Dukhniwaran Sahib Gurudwara



A trip to Patiala is incomplete without a visit to this famous Gurudwara. Experience peace like no other, as you traverse its expansive and embellished courtyards.

Baradari-Neemrana Hotel



The Baradari Palace iderives its name from the twelve door pavilion or baradari, and speaks volumes on hospitality and space.

Patiala

Cinema Halls

There is no dearth of good cinema halls in Patiala. Feel like watching the new horror movie? Or maybe a light hearted rom com? Feel free to head to either of the following:

  • Phul cinema, Fountain Chowk, Choti Baradari
  • Omaxe mall, Mall road, Baradari
While the Phul cinema has been recently renovated and may offer a better cinematic experience, Omaxe mall has the added convenience of excellent food outlets both in and near the mall.



Patiala

Let’s go shopping


Capture Patiala’s vibrance in the local markets: AC Market, Dharampura Bazaar, Tripuri Market, Qila chowk, Anardana chowk, Lahori Gate market, Book market, Sheranwala Gate, Gud mandi etc. Girls, make sure to buy a traditional Phulkari dupatta! You won’t regret this addition to your wardrobe!
However, for shopaholics looking for branded clothing and footwear, head to Omaxe mall, Leela Bhawan or Model Town without a second thought.
