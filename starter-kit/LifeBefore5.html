Classes

Classes



Freshers, Welcome to the place where students are allowed to have mobile phones in the classroom. But it’s a different situation now, ironically classes will be conducted on the phone! Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, classes are currently being conducted online. They are conducted on Zoom meetings according to the time table allotted to the batches which can be accessed through the Thapar Institute- Learning Management System (LMS) portal.
All the subjects are divided into three categories- Lectures (L), Tutorials (T) and Labs (P).
Lectures cover the theory part, labs are for the practical application of the subject and tutorials are the question solving time.
Students are advised to keep track of their attendance because they are supposed to maintain an overall attendance of 75% in each subject.
So, despite the current situation, enjoy the classes, learn something new and don’t forget to keep that attendance in check!!

Resources

Know your online resources

TIET-LMS

LMS is the Learning Management System of Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology through which the Institute conducts online classes and communicates the course content to the students. The students are required to login at the Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology’s LMS cluster server through a browser using the login credentials provided by the Institute or using the Moodle App, by entering the site (Visit Thapar LMS). Once logged in, they can view the detailed information about their courses and subject teachers. Latest announcements, information about the online classes, their Zoom links and the course material is updated there time and again.



Time Table

Reading the timetable:

  • The timetable is provided in the form of an excel sheet.
  • All days except Sunday will be working.
  • Online classes will be held between 08:00AM and 06:00PM.
  • L, T, & P represent Lecture, Tutorial & Practical respectively.
  • Each teacher will have their unique code.
  • Classrooms are also mentioned in the timetable representing where the class will be held.
  • Each subject has a unique subject code.
  • The blank space in a time table represents a break.


Web Kiosk

It is the place where all your college data is stored by the Institute including past, present and future information. It gives you an easy access to various information about your timetable, subject details, professors, hostel status, etc. It includes your LMS information, your fee detail, your marks and also your CGPA.
